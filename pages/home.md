Every Warrant Officer Candidate School class earns a nickname. We are the **War Hares**. The battle-tested, technically elite, and notoriously elusive fighters. Some of us have tattoos of our mascot on our butt cheeks.

WOCS 24-001 graduated in Frankfort, KY in 2024. This is our history, told the way it deserves to be: with just enough hubris and just enough irreverence to honor what we actually went through.

*Standing tall, towering above all, the war hare is prepared to swiftly unleash the "mad as a march hare" ferocity when the wolf growls at the door - because he's been there, done that, and is always ready for more.*
